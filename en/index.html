IT training from professionals to professionals

What do we do?

Nowadays, with all the available technologies and frameworks, a company could devote too much effort to analyze, compare, and finally decide what is the best technical stack to build their projects, or develop their business.

Our consulting and training services can help your company in this task. We have over 15 years experience in development and systems, and about 40,000 hours of training courses delivered in over 1,200 training events for IT industry professionals.

How do we do it?

We use the languages, platforms and tools that are present in companies, to deploy, enhance or support business processes.

Theory is everywhere, so we try to teach in a practical way, with examples and solutions. Often we start our course with an use case or an user story of the company itself, and after we develop it in the classroom.

Where are we?

We have been speakers in last editions of the Greach, and the Codemotion and we've participated in many other events in the developer comunity: past events presence.

We have been sponsors of Spring I/O 2015 , Greach 2015 and Greach 2016.

We are also sponsors of Greach 2017 and the Hackathon Lovers comunity.

Where are we going?

Our main work is to provide quality training to IT professionals. We are focused in Spain, but our goal is to do the same in the rest of Europe.

We have contributed sporadically at events and projects, and we want to increase this contributions in the future. Also we want to offer in an open way, the material of our courses to anyone interested.

About us

We are a group of technical experts, surrounded by very good collaborators, who after participating in multiple technical projects, now we teach and give support services to technology companies or IT departments.

We are passionate about our work: we love the application development, the servers administration and the systems management, and we do it all with agile methodologies.
